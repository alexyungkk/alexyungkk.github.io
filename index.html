About Me

Hi everyone! My name is Ka Kang Yung, but most people call me Alex. I moved to Halifax, Nova Scotia from Hong Kong in 2021, and I’m grateful to call this beautiful and friendly city my new home. I’m also a proud father of two wonderful sons, aged eleven and seven, who love living in Canada and enjoy the kindness of people here.

In my free time, I enjoy watching soccer matches, which is one of my favourite ways to relax. I also love travelling, especially to Japan, where I’m always inspired by its culture and creativity.
